    Performance Indicators: Access

    Teachers' consistent efforts in conducting parent-teacher dialogues and home visitations contributed significantly to lowering the dropout rate from 1.56 in SY 2020-2021 to 1.01 in SY 2021-2022. Dropping out is still primarily due to family issues followed by a lack of interest.

    Performance Indicators: Quality

    For School Year 2021-2022, the promotion rate increased by 2.28%. It is assumed that students have already adjusted to the new normal setting compared to the first year of distance learning.In the last two school years, Mathematics got the lowest Level of Assessment results. At present, the department is intensifying its intervention activities through Project ABC (Accuracy in Basic Computation).

    Performance Indicators: Governance

    With 1387 students and 54 teachers, the school has an average learner-teacher ratio of 26:1 which indicates that there are 26 students for every one teacher. This only shows that the school has enough number of teachers to handle the learners.
